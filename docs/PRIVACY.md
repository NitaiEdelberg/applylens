# Privacy & Data

ApplyLens is a personal/portfolio project. This note explains, plainly, what happens to your data.

## Anonymous by default
If you don't sign in, your CV, job descriptions, and analyses are kept **only in your browser** (localStorage). They are **never stored on our servers**, and clearing your browser data removes them.

## AI processing
The text you submit is sent to our AI provider (**Groq**) to generate the analysis (extraction, fit scoring, tailoring, and the grounding checks). ApplyLens does **not** persist that text. The provider processes it under its own policies.

## Optional accounts
If you create an account (to sync your tracker across devices), we store:
- your **email address** and a securely hashed password, and
- the **applications you choose to save** — title, company, status, and the saved analysis payload.

You can **delete any saved application at any time**, and you can request **account deletion**. We store the minimum needed to provide the sync feature.

## What we don't do
- We **don't sell or share** your data.
- There are **no third-party trackers or ads**.

## As-is
ApplyLens is provided **“as is,” without warranty**. It is a demonstration project, not a commercial service.
